A starter blog

2022-03-30
1 minute read
notes
Blog

I have been reading a lot more after I left my previous job at NortonLifeLock as an SRE. It is nice to catch up with all the technology and tools that I was not using before. The day has only so many hours and at some point I was just burned out and too tired to keep going. Moreover, I still felt like after all these years I have not been sharing much of my thoughts and experiences with anyone other than close friends and family. So I decided to create this to expand that network. I mean, I am not a professional blogger by any stretch and I certainly don’t have the skills to write as other people that have been doing this for years, but…

Don’t you want to take a leap of faith? Or become an old man, filled with regret, waiting to die alone!1

– Saito, Inception (2010)
